Frequently Asked Questions

What can I do to enhance the performance of my air conditioner?

Regular maintenance, timely filter changes, and proper insulation can improve efficiency.

For how long can an air conditioner usually last?

With proper maintenance, most air conditioning systems can last 10-15 years.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we provide flexible financing plans for installations and repairs.

How often should I service my air conditioner?

It's recommended to service your air conditioner at least once a year.

What should I do if my air conditioner leaks water?

Turn off your system and contact us immediately. It may indicate a clogged drain line.

What are signs that my air conditioner needs repair?

Unusual noises, weak airflow, or frequent cycling suggest your system needs attention.
